Getting an electronic appliance repaired is an activity that most people tend to avoid as it is a hassle. To make our lives comfortable, electronic appliances are a must have in every household. There are quite a few appliances which are installed at our homes and offices to make our day to day activities easier. Manufacturing electronics has become very costly & affording a new appliance is not pocket friendly. So, instead of throwing away an old appliance, get it repaired by us. Your appliance will be as good as new.

Every household has several electronic appliances and an average person spends 90% of their time using those appliances. No matter how careful you are while using those appliances, it is inevitable that its functionality reduces with time. Regular maintenance is needed to fix or repair those appliances. Just to take care of these issues, we have come up with repairing electronic appliances at your door step. This in turn means you will not need to carry heavy appliances to any shops, but instead an expert will come to your home or office to fix the appliance.

When it comes to repairing any appliance, we are your most reliable option. From washing machine, refrigerator, TV, microwave to A/C etc, we offer 30 days free service and at no extra cost. Our technicians are highly professional and take all safety measures before stepping into your home. We assure you provide 100% satisfaction with our service.

We make sure to understand your issue with the appliance, as you have been using it for a while.
